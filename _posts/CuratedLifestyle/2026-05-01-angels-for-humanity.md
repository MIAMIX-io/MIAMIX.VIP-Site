## **Tea for a Cause Brings a Garden of Versailles Theme to the Biltmore**

On Sunday, April 26, 2026, Angels for Humanity hosted its annual Tea for a Cause at the iconic Biltmore Hotel in Coral Gables, welcoming a distinguished community for an afternoon where elegance and purpose came together with intention. Now in its fifth year, the event introduced a Gardens of Versailles theme, thoughtfully woven into the setting to create a refined and immersive experience within the hotel.

From the moment guests arrived, the atmosphere reflected a careful balance of beauty and meaning. The Versailles inspired elements were present in the details, enhancing the space without overwhelming it and creating an environment that felt both elevated and inviting. It was a setting designed not just to impress, but to support a greater purpose.

At the heart of the afternoon was the mission of Angels for Humanity, an organization dedicated to uplifting underserved children through initiatives focused on education, healthcare, and essential resources. Tea for a Cause serves as an extension of that work, offering a platform where community and philanthropy intersect in a meaningful way.

Guests enjoyed delectable bites and champagne as the experience unfolded with a refined sense of ease and intention. A highlight of the afternoon was the fashion presentation by Giannina Azar, whose designs brought an added layer of artistry and sophistication to the event, seamlessly complementing the theme and elevating the overall experience.

Each element was thoughtfully integrated, allowing the event to flow naturally while maintaining a clear sense of purpose. Guests were not only present for the experience, but engaged in the impact behind it.

Beyond the event itself, Angels for Humanity continues to create lasting change through a range of initiatives. Back to school health fairs provide children with uniforms, supplies, and medical care, while holiday toy distributions bring moments of joy to families during the season. Internationally, their efforts extend to Haiti and underserved communities across the Caribbean and South America, where medical missions deliver critical services, often for the first time.

What defined this year’s gathering was the collective intention of those in the room. Leaders, philanthropists, and community members came together with a shared understanding that true luxury lies in impact. The conversations, the presence, and the support all contributed to something far greater than the afternoon itself.

In a city known for its vibrant social calendar, Tea for a Cause continues to stand apart. It is not about scale, but about substance. Not about spectacle, but about purpose.

As the event concluded, what remained was a sense of alignment between elegance and impact, an experience that reflected the values of Angels for Humanity and the community it continues to inspire.
