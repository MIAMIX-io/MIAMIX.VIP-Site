App Support

Everything you need to know about the MIAMIX App. Find answers to common questions below, or reach out to our team directly.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is MIAMIX App?
The MIAMIX app is your ultimate digital concierge, providing exclusive access to curated lifestyle features, VIP updates, and premium content across Miami's hottest channels including sports, fashion, and hospitality.
Is the app free to download?
Yes! The core features of the MIAMIX app are entirely free to download and use. We may offer premium VIP tiers for exclusive event access in the future.
How do I reset my password?
Open the app and navigate to the login screen. Tap on "Forgot Password," enter your registered email address, and follow the instructions sent to your inbox to securely reset your credentials.
Can I customize my content feed?
Absolutely. Under your profile settings, you can select your favorite channels (such as Culture & Art or Health & Wellness) to tailor your home feed exactly to your interests.
The app is crashing or freezing. What should I do?
First, ensure you are running the latest version of the app from the App Store or Google Play. If the issue persists, try forcing the app to close and clearing your cache. If you still need help, please contact our support team below.
