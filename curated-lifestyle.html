Curated Lifestyle
by Romina

In a world where trends change overnight, Romina Nabhen stands as a steady, conscious voice deeply connected to the essence of true luxury. A Luxury Brand Strategist, media personality, Founder and CEO of RCN Curated Luxury, Romina has dedicated over twenty-seven years to helping brands and individuals tell stories with heart, beauty, and an elegance that transcends fleeting trends.

Her journey began in modeling, a stage that allowed her to discover the power of visual narrative and the transformative capacity of style. Those early years in front of the camera gave her an intuitive understanding of how image, authenticity, and presence intertwine to create impact. Over time, Romina brought those learnings into television, editorial, and corporate realms, developing a unique strategic vision within the luxury universe.

Meet Romina

In every chapter of her career, Romina has demonstrated an exceptional ability to unite creativity and business from a sensitive, results-oriented approach. That philosophy led her to found RCN Curated Luxury, where she offers business development, strategic partnerships, art direction, style consulting, media training, and editorial storytelling for a select group of global clients. Her mission is clear: create visibility with soul and credibility with purpose, ensuring that every brand reflects not only excellence but essence.

"True luxury is not about excess. It's about alignment, about the quiet confidence of knowing who you are and curating everything around you from that truth."

This vision is reflected in every project she leads, where intention is always as important as aesthetics.

Through her podcast My LIFE | Mi VIDA on Spotify and her project The Seasonal Review, Romina invites her audience into a space where style, reflection, and sensitivity coexist naturally. Her partnership collaboration with MIAMIX contributes special articles focused on Fashion, Beauty & Lifestyle, aligned with her contemporary and deeply human perspective on luxury.

Her presence also stands out in women's leadership initiatives. As Platform Partner and CEO Circle Member of Miami Women Who Rock, Romina is part of a community dedicated to inspiration, philanthropy, and empowerment. She was recently featured on Telemundo National as an expert voice on reinvention, and profiled in The Book of Rockstar Leaders, a book recognizing visionary entrepreneurs driving Miami's development.

"Luxury is how you live between the big milestones: in conversations, in connections, and in those quiet moments you choose to make beautiful."

Today, Romina Nabhen continues expanding her impact with projects, collaborations, and experiences that celebrate the beauty of living with intention. Whether through a global strategy, an elegant event, or an intimate conversation, her work reflects what she embodies: purpose, art, and an elegance that endures.
